about us

webforge is a non-profit that intends to support and advocate for the causes of other non-profit organizations through the creation of free, hand-coded websites.

Our technology stack includes HTML5, CSS, JS, TS, React, Next.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and many more.

meet the team

Our team is comprised of passionate student web developers, who wish to devote their skills to support your cause. Hover over a team member's image to learn more about them.
