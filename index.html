about us

webforge is a non-profit digital agency that intends to support and advocate for the causes of other non-profit organizations through the creation of free, hand-coded websites.

Our technology stack includes HTML5, CSS, JS, TS, React, Next.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and many more.
